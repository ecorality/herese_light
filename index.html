Why HERESE

We make cravings useful.

A craving is often not random. Around periods and hormonal shifts, the body can ask for sweetness, chocolate, salt, cooling drinks, or something warm and grounding.

Sweet cravings can show up when mood and energy dip. Chocolate cravings are common during periods and can feel like the body is asking for mineral-rich comfort. Salty cravings can appear with bloating, fluid shifts, and changing hydration needs.

HERESE starts there. We keep the taste women already want, then build each drink with herbs, minerals, and phase-specific nutrition.

The idea is simple: satisfy the craving, and support the body need behind it.
